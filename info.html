Ferd Leimkuhler Memorial Celebration

When

Friday, June 12, 2026, Noon to 2pm

Where

The Brazilian Room in Tilden Park, on the eastern edge of Berkeley, California.

Food & Drink

There will be comestibles and libations.

RSVP

Let us know who's coming by emailing Natalie at natleim@gmail.com.

Streaming

We intend to stream the event live and make it available for later viewing. Check back here for details.

If you can't attend and want to share something about Ferd, send a video! Here are some guidelines:

  • Record in horizontal format; If using a phone, turn it sideways
  • Make sure a light source is facing you and is not behind you
  • Record indoors in a quiet place
  • Try to keep it under five minutes long
  • If your video file is under 25MB you can just attach it to an email; If your file is larger, upload it to a cloud service (e.g. Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud) and share the link in an email
  • Send your video to kristin.leimkuhler@gmail.com by Sunday, May 31st

Driving Directions

The Brazilian Room is located in Tilden Regional Park at the intersection of Wildcat Canyon and Shasta Roads.
View on Google Maps

☞ Note! There is little or no cellular reception at the Brazilian Room, so plan ahead!

From Highway 80:

Take University Avenue exit in Berkeley
Follow University until it ends at Oxford St.
Left on Oxford to Rose St.
Right on Rose one block to Spruce St.
Left on Spruce and go about 1.5 miles to the stop sign at Grizzly Peak Blvd.
Continue straight across Grizzly Peak Blvd. and Spruce becomes Wildcat Canyon Road
Continue for two miles and the Brazilian Room will be on your left

From Highway 24 heading west:

Before Caldecott Tunnel, exit Fish Ranch Road and follow it up to the stop sign at Grizzly Peak Boulevard
Turn right on Grizzly Peak and go around 2.5 miles to the stop sign at Golf Course Road
Right on Golf Course Rd. and continue to the stop sign at Shasta Road
Right on Shasta
Shasta Road ends at Wildcat Canyon Rd. and the Brazilian Room parking lot

Parking

There is a parking area directly in front of the Brazilian Room.
You can also park along Wildcat Canyon Rd.
Parking is also allowed on the shoulder of Anza View Rd.
